What's happening at the counter

The counter is where
brands lose control.

A rep walks into a shop with a thousand products, a price list, and their own judgment. What actually gets pitched, at what price, with which message — nobody upstream can see or control it. Orders come back on paper. Invoices get typed by hand.

Off the

Reps pitch what they like, in the order they like. The brand's plan stops at the warehouse.

Unseen

Paper &
guesswork

What Beat is

One tablet runs
the whole visit.

Beat is a guided, brand-controlled sales tool on a tablet — paired with a web console that controls every catalog, price and message behind it. One source of truth, from head office to the shop floor.

On the tablet

The Rep App

A locked, brand-led sequence: pitch each product on a designed card, play the ad, take the order at a protected price, and send a proforma on WhatsApp — online or off.

On the web

The Admin Console

Products, price tiers and floors, the catalog order, the ad breaks and the branded cards — set once, centrally, and pushed to every rep, region by region.

Two surfaces · one source of truth03
